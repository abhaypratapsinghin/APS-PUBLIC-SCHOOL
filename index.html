About us


APS PUBLIC SCHOOL has covered a journey of 5 years, and has covered a sea of achievements during it. The milestone that has been achieved has led to the school being acclaimed as one of the best CBSE schools and under the mentorship of the Principal, Mr. A.P. Singh the school has reached the pinnacle of excellence. Academics, Arts, Music, Sports, Quiz, Debates, and Dramatics etc. are the areas in which the rich potential of talents are seen in abundance with the school touching the heights of recognition by winning accolades and getting kudos. The healthy environment and modern infrastructure reinstates the opinion that APS PUBLIC SCHOOL is poised for the ultimate excellence and name.


Education Under Secured Environment

To ensure safety and security of our children we have established a system which keeps the parents informed about the absenteeism of the child from school. SMS alerts are communicated to parents for their information and conveniences.

Best environment

We have a huge array of subjects and disciplines to offer, tailor made for the learner’s interest and aptitude. The school is based at a place away from the humdrum of the city, in the lap of nature where one can spot a variety of avians, it is situated in a historically important heritage spot as well.

Best labs

We have a top level of labs such as Biology lab, Computer lab, etc. with one of the facilities and safety kits. We don't take risk in our children life.
