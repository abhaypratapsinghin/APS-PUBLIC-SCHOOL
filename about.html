About us



From 110 or so students to more than 4000. The school has earned a recognition, regionally, zonally and at national level. We aim to proceed and flourish, to forge ahead. Nothing can be done without hope and confidence (which we have in abundance).
The spirit of support and strength is imparted to one and all by Mr. Amin – The Pro – Vice Chairman whose unstinted support has made the school have the solid edifice on which rests its pinnacle, striding from strength to strength. The school under the leadership of its Principal and the strong role of management has covered a long journey and will touch many more milestones.
Mrs. Nigam credibility is unmatched as the mentor leader and guide of the school. The “Pratibha Sammaan” and “Excellence Awards” were given to Mr. A.P. Singh consecutively for 2 years.
APS PUBLIC SCHOOL received the International School Award by British Council. He was also recognized as one the most effective principals out of the cluster, not only that, the school received the award as one of the best 10 CBSE schools. Also the World Education School had ranked the school as one of the best in terms of the performance and leadership.
The infrastructure and discipline of the school speak volumes of its spirit invoked by the head, of the institution. No matter what the event, the moral values and overall development resound in the school and the norms it stands for.
To sum up we can say about the spirit of APS PUBLIC SCHOOL is “always keep your face to the sunshine, and you cannot see the shadow”.
